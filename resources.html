Resources

Just like taking care of your physical health is important, taking care of your mental health is critical. It will take patience, practice and support.

Some of the advice to maintain/improve your mental health:
- Understand that life can be challenging.
- Understand your strengths and weaknesses.
- Plan realistic goals.
- Understand yourself and others.
- Understand that you and others have both positive and negative feelings.
- Build a sense of meaning in your life by learning and trying new activities/hobbies.
- Build healthy, trusting relationships.
